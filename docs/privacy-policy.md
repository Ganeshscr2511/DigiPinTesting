# 🔐 Privacy Policy

**Department of Posts, Government of India**  
Version: 1.0

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the Department of Posts, Government of India ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and processes information through the **Know Your DIGIPIN** application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

The Know Your DIGIPIN application is designed to fetch DIGIPIN grid value codes based on location.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data
When you use the application, we collect your geographical location data (latitude and longitude) based on your consent to provide accurate DIGIPIN grid information.

- This data is **not stored for future use**
- No personal identifiers are linked to this data

---

### 2.2 Technical Information
We may collect:
- Device type  
- Browser information  
- IP address  

This is used only to ensure proper functioning of the application.

---

### 2.3 Cookies
We use cookies only for:
- Human verification (security purposes)

These cookies:
- Do NOT track user behavior  
- Are essential for preventing misuse  

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use data to:
- Provide accurate DIGIPIN codes  
- Improve application functionality  
- Maintain system security  
- Analyze performance and usage  

---

## 4. Information Sharing

We do **NOT**:
- Sell  
- Rent  
- Trade  

your data with third parties.

We may disclose information:
- If required by law  
- To authorized government personnel  

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement:

- Encryption of data in transit  
- Secure access controls  
- Regular security assessments  

---

## 6. Your Choices

You can:
- Deny location access via device settings  

⚠️ This may limit functionality.

---

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy for:
- Legal reasons  
- Operational improvements  

Updated versions will be published with a revised date.
